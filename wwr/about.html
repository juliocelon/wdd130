History

Founded in 2025 by a group of adventure enthusiasts, our company began with a single raft and a dream to share the thrill of whitewater rafting with the world. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted leader in outdoor adventures, guiding thousands of guests down some of the most breathtaking rivers. With a deep commitment to safety, sustainability, and creating unforgettable memories, we continue to inspire a love for the great outdoors.
